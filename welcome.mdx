The current version of the Pelm API is in the early stages of development. There will be bugs; if you catch any, please email them to [info@pelm.com](mailto:info@pelm.com), and we will fix them within 72 hours.

You can view the list of [supported utilities](/reference/utilities). If there's a utility that you need data from and would like to see next, get in contact with us at [info@pelm.com](mailto:info@pelm.com), and we'll be happy to chat.
